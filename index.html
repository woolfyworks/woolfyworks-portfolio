Welcome to WOOLFYWORKS~!

Operating out of sunny Florida since 2018, with a focus on handmade craft and costume, WoolfyWorks is an art brand which aims to spread good vibes and cartoon animal art of all kinds! Through conventions/markets, costume performance, and the WoolfyWorks Etsy Storefront, (Formerly “Fluffywolfstudios”) creating unique and playful art has always been the guiding wish.

Since the beginning, crafting characters to life through art and costumes under the “Xiolet Woolfy” name has been the goal. WoolfyWorks could not be complete without “Fursuits”, the one of a kind animal costumes that are the spirit for the style and flare for self expression unique to those in the Furry Fandom. WoolfyWorks's mascot, Koda the Woolf Pup, is a playful representation of the brand, and an example of how one can express themselves through creative costumes & unique apparel! Thank you for visting, Enjoy~!
