WoolfyWorks Furuits

Dedicated to Style, Quality, Comfort & Fun!


Price Guide Located Here~

WoolfyWorks has been focused on the fursuit making craft since 2018. Starting off working with others foam bases, I eventually moved on to create my own foam work, along with larger parts like feetpaws and bodysuits. The focus of my costume work is dedicated to 4 pillars; affordability, quality, style, and comfort! I want my fursuits to be remain accessible in price range, alongside continuing to develop better techniques and innovate aspects of my craft and commission process! Currently, I am practicing my skill building, quality, and turnaround times with premade work in order to create the best experience for custom commissions in the future!



Any fursuit commissions or premade drops will be announced through social media (primarily xiolet.woolfy instagram) and listed on Etsy: WoolfyWorks (if not preclaimed)
