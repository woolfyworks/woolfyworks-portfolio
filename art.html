About the artist:

Xiolet Woolfy
Aka “Xio” “Woolfy” “Ame”

Hello! I’m Xiolet I am a 19 year old cartoon artist from the US, and a member of the Furry Fandom since 2018. My fursona is a corgi/wolf hybrid named Xiolet. Some big inspirations behind my art are anime, nature, and of course the Furry Fandom. I am currently studying game design and 3D modeling at University while running my Etsy Shop and sometimes take general art commissions through social media. Some of my art passions include 2D and digital drawing, sculpting, costume and cosplay creation, live 2D rigging, and merch design/production and a lot more! Outside art I enjoy nature, anime, coding, long-boarding and of course fursuiting~!
